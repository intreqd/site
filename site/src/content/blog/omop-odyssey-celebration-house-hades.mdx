After our journey through the technical landscapes of Troy, we've arrived at a moment of celebration. We've successfully bridged the clinical world with the analytical power of the OMOP Common Data Model using InterSystems IRIS. Welcome to the **House of Hades**, the foundational layer where our transformed data now resides, ready for deep discovery.

### The Foundation: InterSystems IRIS & OMOP

The integration between InterSystems IRIS and OMOP isn't just about moving data—it's about creating a robust, high-performance foundation for healthcare analytics.

![InterSystems IRIS & OMOP Integration](https://community.intersystems.com/sites/default/files/inline/images/images/image(10670).png)

### Navigating the House of Hades

In the OMOP world, the "House of Hades" (HADES) is actually a collection of open-source R packages for population-level characterization, patient-level prediction, and population-level estimation.

![OHDSI HADES Overview](https://community.intersystems.com/sites/default/files/inline/images/images/image(10671).png)
