Managed Workload

What exactly is a Managed Workload?

Our customers typically come to us to ease the onboarding of workloads that are outside of their current solutions, require cloud based horse power to achieve, and typically require some subject expertise to get data onboarded, where not necessarily operationalizing the entire solution is top of mind.

We have customers comfortable on their own infrastructure, that need a data processing competency that is taxes their compute footprint just for the sake of normalizing vast amounts of source data either on-demand, or on a schedule and need immutability to the stack.

We have customers that are masters of their own tech stack, that are sometimes confronted with a commercial third party off the shelf product that makes more sense than custom development, and do not want to distract their development staff with implementing it.

We have customers that need workloads remediated for compliance attestation, and operational alignment with audit and Data Request Fulfilment for the continual adherence to privacy practices for their customers.
